Training


At Dragon Tattoo Salon we offer a unique opportunity to learn and
improve your skills in the art of tattooing through our master class. We are
proud to share our knowledge nd experience with those who aspire to
become a professional tattoo artist or develop their existing skills.





Our master class provides a structured training program that covers all
aspects of tattooing, from basic principles and techniques to more complex
styles and designs. Our experienced and talented artisans will share their
secrets and best practices to help you become a confident and competent
tattoo artist.
