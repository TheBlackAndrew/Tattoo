Sketches and ordering your own

Dragon Tattoo Salon is pleased to offer a wide range of ready-made designs that can be the inspiration and starting point for your future tattoo. Our talented artists carefully develop each sketch to offer you a variety of options to suit different styles and preferences.

At Dragon Tattoo Salon we offer the opportunity to order your own sketch for your future tattoo. We understand that each client is unique and has their own individual preferences, so we strive to create art that reflects your uniqueness and style.



When you decide to commission your own sketch, our team of professional artists and tattoo artists work closely with you to fully understand your ideas, preferences and tattoo concept. We value your creativity and offer you our expert help to turn your ideas into visually appealing art on your skin.
